DUTIES

A Senior Safety Engineer Pressure Vessels participates in and/or assigns, reviews and evaluates the work of Safety Engineers Pressure Vessels engaged in inspecting fired and unfired pressure vessels, and appurtenances for conformance to State laws and City ordinances regulating their design, fabrication, installation and operation; applies sound supervisory principles and techniques in building and maintaining an effective work force; and fulfills equal employment opportunity responsibilities.

REQUIREMENTS/MINIMUM QUALIFICATIONS

1. Two years of full-time paid experience as a Safety Engineer Pressure Vessels with the City of Los Angeles; and
2. A valid Certification of Competency to inspect boilers and pressure vessels issued by the State of California Department of Industrial Relations.

PROCESS NOTES

1. Applicants who lack six months or less of the required experience may file for this examination. However, they cannot be appointed until the full experience requirement is met.
2. A valid California driver's license is required. Applicants will be disqualified and not eligible for hire if their record within the last 36 months reflects three or more moving violations and/or at-fault accidents, or a conviction of a major moving violation (such as DUI). 
3. Applicants must submit a valid Certificate of Competency to inspect boilers and pressure vessels issued by the State of California Department of Industrial Relations. Applicants must attach a copy of the Certificate of Competency to the on-line application at the time of filing. The certificate must be attached to the on-line application BEFORE the application is submitted. If the certificate is attached AFTER the on-line application has been submitted, it will not be available to the City and applicants WILL NOT be credited with having submitted the certificate. Applicants who fail to submit the Certificate of Competency at the time of filing by attaching it to the on-line application will not be considered further in this examination. 
4. Positions in the Los Angeles Department of Water and Power require a Repair (R) and an Owner-User (O) Endorsement from the National Board of Boiler and Pressure Vessel Inspectors by the completion of the probationary period.
5. Upon appointment, a Senior Safety Engineer Pressure Vessels may be required to furnish an automobile, properly insured, for use in City service. Mileage will be paid on the basis of established rates.

SELECTION PROCESS

Examination Weight:  Interview . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . 100%

The examination will consist entirely of an evaluation of experience and personal qualifications by interview. In the interview, emphasis will be placed on the candidate's experience, training, and professional development as they provide the knowledge of: boilers and pressure vessels manufacture installation, operation and maintenance, repairs, and alterations; methods of techniques of inspecting boilers and pressure vessels regarding repairs and alterations; state laws, city ordinances, American Society of Mechanical Engineers codes, National Board Inspection Code and department requirements related to the fabrication, installation, operation, repair, alteration, and maintenance of boilers and pressure vessels; engineering principles and practices applicable to the design, repair, alteration and construction of boilers and other pressure vessels; welding and riveting and non-destructive examination, and techniques related to the manufacture, repair, alteration, and installation of boilers and pressure vessels; department and division policies, procedures and functions; supervisory principles and practices, including equal employment opportunity responsibilities; supervision, management and organization principles; and the ability to: detect unsafe conditions in boiler and pressure vessels and recommend corrective action; interpret plans and specifications; communicate effectively both orally and in writing; deal tactfully and effectively with others; and other necessary knowledge, skills, and abilities.

Candidates will be notified later by e-mail of the time, date, and location of the interview, which will be held in Los Angeles. It is anticipated that interviews will begin during the period of DECEMBER 17, 2018 to DECEMBER 28, 2018.
